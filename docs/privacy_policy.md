# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 2025-11-26

## 1. Information We Collect

TeamFlow Command Center ("we", "us", "our") collects the following information to provide our services:

- **User Information**: Name, email address, and user ID from Zoho Projects and Zoho Cliq.
- **Task Data**: Task names, descriptions, due dates, priorities, and status from Zoho Projects.
- **Usage Data**: Interaction logs with our Cliq extension (commands, widget views).

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

- Calculate team capacity and workload.
- Provide smart task assignment recommendations.
- Send notifications and alerts regarding capacity.
- Display personalized priority feeds.

## 3. Data Retention

- We retain cached data (capacity, priorities) for up to 30 days to improve performance.
- You can request data deletion by uninstalling the extension or contacting support.

## 4. Data Security

- All data is stored securely in Zoho Catalyst DataStore.
- OAuth tokens are stored in encrypted Catalyst Secrets.
- We do not share your data with third parties.

## 5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@teamflow.com.
